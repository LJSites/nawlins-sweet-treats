— Meet Cynthia —

The real deal,
nine miles from Crenshaw.

Cynthia bakes the king cakes Los Angeles families used to fly in from New Orleans. Yeast dough. Cinnamon crunch running through the spiral. Purple, gold, and green colored sugar. The real thing.

She'll deck a cake for your Mardi Gras party, your daughter's birthday, your office, your church potluck — communicative, flexible, and always on time. Order ahead. She'll take care of the rest.
